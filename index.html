I'm a Cognitive Neuroscience PhD student advised by Emily Finn in the Functional Imaging and Naturalistic Neuroscience Lab at Dartmouth College.

Most recently, I've been teaching extended learning classes which I've been really enjoying!


Research interests

Broadly, I am interested in the intersection between experiences (defined broadly) and cognition. One of the questions I'm interested in is how do individuals with a shared experience reach variable memories, conclusions or interpretations about the exact same events? In no particular order, relevant sub-questions for this include:

  1. How do we integrate incoming information with our existent knowledge?
  2. How are neural dynamics and patterns modified and updated?
  3. What is the interaction between past knowledge/memories and incoming sensory information?
  4. How can we identify meaningful individual variability in neural activity that subserves this variability in behavior?
  5. How can we experimentally manipulate or perturb these processes?
