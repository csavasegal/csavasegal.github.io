I'm a Cognitive Neuroscience PhD student advised by Emily Finn in the Functional Imaging and Naturalistic Neuroscience Lab at Dartmouth College.

Most recently, I've been teaching extended learning classes which I've been really enjoying!


Research interests

One of the questions I'm currently interested in is how can individuals with a shared experience reach variable memories, conclusions or interpretations about the exact same events? More broadly, I'm interested in relevant subquestion such as:

  1. What is the interaction between past knowledge/memories and incoming sensory information?
  2. How do we integrate incoming information with our existent knowledge?
  3. How are neural dynamics and patterns modified and updated?
  4. How can we identify meaningful individual variability in neural activity that subserves this variability in behavior?
  5. How can we experimentally manipulate or perturb these processes?
